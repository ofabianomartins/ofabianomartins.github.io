Welcome! I have been thinking about starting this blog for many years, and honestly
this feels like the right moment.
There is a well-known saying: "The best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago;
the second best time is now." If I had started back then and kept investing time and energy,
I might have shade by now—but to write (especially about programming) you need something to say.
That is much more than content alone: you need experience to know what is worth saying.
With this blog I want to give back to the community.
I received a lot of help from people who wrote posts about bugs and features.
I hope to return the favor and use this space to improve how I communicate.
There is a lot to do and a lot to improve. I hope you enjoy the ride!
